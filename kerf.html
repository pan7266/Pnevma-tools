Better result checks

Kerf calibration: Cut a calibration coupon with the same power, speed, air assist, focus, and optical profile before saving production offsets.

9-spot alignment test: Mark low-power dots at near, middle, and far positions across X and Y. If dots drift between corners, correct alignment first or enter the measured alignment loss before trusting focus depth.
